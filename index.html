Welcome to Redivivus

More About Us

Redivivus is company located in Mumbai, India proudly serving individuals and businesses in Mumbai to get rid from their recyclable Waste, accepting recyclable waste in any conditions.

Services

We provide recyclable waste pickup services. After your waste is being picked up, it is send to industrial recycling plants, where your waste is transformed into a building block for a new product.

Convenient Pickup

You can choose the time of pickup according to your convenience and we promise to be punctual to it.
 
 
 

E-waste Events

We work with non-profits, businesses, and other organizations to host community e-waste events.
